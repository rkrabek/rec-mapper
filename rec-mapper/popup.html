Click-Based Selection

Click on 2-3 similar elements, then auto-detect all matching items. Works with text, addresses, and Google Maps links.

How it works:

  1. Click "Start Selection" to enter selection mode
  2. Click on 2-3 similar items on the page
  3. Click "Find Matches" to detect all similar items
  4. Review and exclude false positives
  5. Add a search area (optional) for better geocoding
  6. Click "Map Locations" to view on map in new tab
